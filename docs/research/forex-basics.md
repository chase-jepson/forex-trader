# Forex Basics

Forex trading means exchanging one currency for another. A trade on `EUR/USD` is a view on the euro relative to the US dollar.

If the system buys `EUR/USD`, it benefits when the euro strengthens against the dollar. If it sells `EUR/USD`, it benefits when the euro weakens against the dollar.

The system will not assume a strategy is valid because it sounds plausible. A strategy must first document:

- the market behavior it is trying to capture
- the data it needs
- the entry rule
- the exit rule
- the risk concern that could make the rule fail
